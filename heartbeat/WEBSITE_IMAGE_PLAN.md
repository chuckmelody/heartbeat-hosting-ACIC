# Website Image Plan for Heartbeat A CIC

This document provides a complete list of the images required for the website, based on the content and structure of the application. For each item, a concept is described along with several effective search terms to use on Unsplash or other stock photo services.

---

## 1. Service Images

These images are for the "Our Services" section on the dashboard. They should be professional, positive, and reflect the community-focused nature of each service. A 4:3 aspect ratio (landscape) is recommended to fit the card design.

### a. Community Project Consulting

*   **Concept:** The image should convey collaboration, planning, and professionalism. Think of a diverse group of people working together in a bright, positive setting.
*   **Unsplash Search Terms:**
    *   `team collaborating on a project`
    *   `community meeting planning`
    *   `diverse group working in an office`
    *   `people looking at blueprints together`

### b. Youth Mentorship Programs

*   **Concept:** The image should show guidance, support, and a positive learning environment. Focus on a one-on-one interaction between an adult mentor and a younger person.
*   **Unsplash Search Terms:**
    *   `mentor guiding a student`
    *   `adult helping a teenager with homework`
    *   `positive role model for youth`
    *   `two people talking at a desk bright`

### c. Discipline Through Sport

*   **Concept:** This image should represent focus, determination, and positive physical activity. A martial arts or sports training theme is ideal, emphasizing discipline over aggression.
*   **Unsplash Search Terms:**
    *   `martial arts training focus`
    *   `karate class discipline`
    *   `person training in a gym determination`
    *   `youth sports team huddle`

### d. Community Safety Workshops

*   **Concept:** The image should depict a community gathering, open dialogue, and a sense of trust and collaboration. A town-hall style meeting or a group discussion is a good theme.
*   **Unsplash Search Terms:**
    *   `community event workshop`
    *   `group discussion in a town hall`
    *   `people sitting in a circle talking`
    *   `diverse community gathering indoors`

## 2. Founder & Team Images

These images are for the "Our Founder" section.

### a. Junior Anderson BEM (Main Portrait)

*   **Concept:** A professional and approachable headshot or portrait of the founder, Junior Anderson. This should convey trustworthiness and leadership.
*   **Source:** This will likely be a personal photo rather than a stock image. You will need to acquire a high-quality photograph of Mr. Anderson.

### b. Martial Arts & Accolades

*   **Concept:** An action shot or a formal photo of Junior Anderson in his martial arts capacity (e.g., as a referee, coach, or performing a kata). This image should highlight his expertise and dedication.
*   **Source:** This will also be a personal photo. Look for a high-resolution image from a competition, training session, or awards ceremony.

---

**Recommendation:** When searching on Unsplash, use the "Orientation" filter and select "Landscape" to find images that will best fit the service card design.
